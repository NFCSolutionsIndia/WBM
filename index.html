Impact & Purpose

Transforming Waste Into
Global Impact

We recover critical minerals from electronic waste using AI-powered systems — reducing environmental damage and powering future technologies.

01

The Challenge

Electronic waste is the world's fastest-growing waste stream. Valuable critical minerals are lost to landfill while mining continues to devastate ecosystems and fuel geopolitical conflict.

02

Our Approach

We use AI-powered robotics and hydrometallurgical chemistry to extract and refine critical minerals from multiple e-waste streams — all under one integrated, zero-liquid-discharge facility.

03

The Impact

Reducing landfill, cutting mining dependency, and supplying the critical minerals that power EVs, AI infrastructure, and global industries — creating a true circular economy.

How It Works

From Waste to
World-Class Materials

A five-step AI-powered journey that transforms discarded electronics into critical minerals for tomorrow's industries.

Waste Collection

We partner with corporations, municipalities, and data centres to collect e-waste, batteries, and rare earth magnets. Our logistics network ensures secure, certified pickup with chain-of-custody documentation.

0110,000+ tonnes/yr capacity

AI Sorting

Our proprietary AI vision systems scan, classify, and sort every piece of waste in real-time. Robotic arms work alongside our technicians with 98.7% material identification accuracy.

0298.7% AI accuracy rate

Extraction Process

Using hydrometallurgical and pyrometallurgical processes, we extract precious and critical minerals. Zero liquid discharge technology ensures we're environmentally compliant at every stage.

03Zero liquid discharge

Mineral Formation

Extracted materials are refined to industry-grade purity. Lithium, cobalt, copper, gold, and 40+ other critical minerals are processed and certified for immediate industrial use.

0499.9% purity grade

Global Distribution

Certified minerals are distributed to EV manufacturers, semiconductor companies, defence contractors, and AI infrastructure providers globally through our smart marketplace.

0540+ countries served
