End-to-End Recovery

A precise journey from
trash to treasure

Our vertically integrated recovery lifecycle is engineered for maximum yield, full traceability, and minimal impact.

The Recovery Lifecycle

Step 01

Strategic Collection

We partner with global electronics manufacturers and data centers to collect production scrap and end-of-life hardware.

Step 02

Secure Logistics

Our certified logistics network ensures safe, traceable transportation of materials to our regional processing hubs.

Step 03

Automated Sorting

AI-powered vision systems identify and sort components based on material composition and value density.

Step 04

Precision Dismantling

Robotic systems safely disassemble devices to isolate batteries, logic boards, and display assemblies.

Step 05

Advanced Extraction

Hydrometallurgical and mechanical processes extract precious and rare earth elements with high precision.

Step 06

Final Refinement

Recovered materials are refined to industrial-grade purity, ready to be reintegrated into the supply chain.
